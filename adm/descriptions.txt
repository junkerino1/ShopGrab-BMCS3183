Database Components Description

--- Teammate 1 ---
Procedures:
- Login customer: Authenticates and logs in a customer.
- Register customer: Registers a new customer into the database.
- Claim voucher: Processes the logic for a customer claiming a specific voucher.

Triggers:
- After customer insert: Executes necessary initialization logic immediately after a new customer is added (e.g., wallet or loyalty tracking creation).
- Before voucher insert: Validates or modifies voucher data before it is officially inserted into the database.

Reports:
- Coin flow report: Generates a report on monthly coins issued and redeemed based on each loyalty tier.
- Coin transaction report: Generates a report detailing the historical coin transaction records for customers.

--- Teammate 2 ---
Procedures:
- Prepare order: Updates an order's status to preparing and processes related initial order details.
- Apply discount: Calculates and applies applicable voucher or loyalty discounts to an order's total.

Triggers:
- After voucher update: Executes logic after a voucher is modified (e.g., tracking claims or redemption status).
- Before loyalty update: Validates or prepares data before a customer's loyalty tier or point balance changes.

Reports:
- Voucher countdown report: Displays vouchers that are approaching their expiration dates.
- Voucher redemption report: Details the usage and redemption statistics of various vouchers.

--- Teammate 3 ---
Procedures:
- Create payment: Initializes and processes a payment transaction for a customer order.
- Finalize order: Completes the order confirmation process and moves it to the appropriate queue.

Triggers:
- After order insert: Executes logic immediately after a new order is placed (e.g., notifying restaurant/rider).
- After payment insert: Executes logic after a payment is successfully recorded (e.g., updating order status).

Reports:
- Rider current delivery report: Shows the active deliveries that are currently being handled by riders.
- Rider delivery report: Details the overall historical delivery performance and records of riders.

--- Teammate 4 ---
Procedures:
- Add subscription: Assigns and activates a new subscription for a customer.
- Add to cart: Inserts selected menu items into a customer's active shopping cart.

Triggers:
- After sub payment insert: Executes logic after a subscription payment is completed (e.g., extending subscription dates).
- Before cart insert: Validates cart items (e.g., checking item availability) before they are added.

Reports:
- Monthly restaurant sales: Shows the aggregated monthly sales revenue for the restaurants.
- Subscription countdown report: Displays customers with a subscription that is ending in 7 days.

--- Teammate 5 ---
Procedures:
- Complete order (Complete delivery): Marks an order as successfully delivered and finalized.
- Add rider: Registers a new delivery rider into the system.

Triggers:
- Before rider update: Validates or tracks changes before a rider's information (like status or location) is updated.
- After rider insert: Executes logic immediately after a new rider is added to the system.

Reports:
- Customer retention report: Analyzes and displays customer retention metrics and return rates.
- Average order value report: Calculates total spending and average spending of customers, grouped by their loyalty tier.
