Database Components Description

--- Teammate 1 ---
Procedures:
- Login customer: Authenticates and logs in a customer.
- Register customer: Registers a new customer into the database.
- Claim voucher: Processes the logic for a customer claiming a specific voucher.

Triggers:
- After customer insert: Executes necessary initialization logic immediately after a new customer is added (e.g., wallet or loyalty tracking creation).
- Before voucher insert: Validates or modifies voucher data before it is officially inserted into the database.

Reports:
- Coin flow report: Generates a report on monthly coins issued and redeemed based on each loyalty tier.
- Coin transaction report: Generates a report detailing the historical coin transaction records for customers.

--- Teammate 2 ---
Procedures:
- Prepare order: Updates an order's status to preparing and processes related initial order details.
- Apply discount: Calculates and applies applicable voucher or loyalty discounts to an order's total.

Triggers:
- After voucher update: Executes logic after a voucher is modified (e.g., tracking claims or redemption status).
- Before loyalty update: Validates or prepares data before a customer's loyalty tier or point balance changes.

Reports:
- Voucher countdown report: Displays vouchers that are approaching their expiration dates.
- Voucher redemption report: Details the usage and redemption statistics of various vouchers.

--- Teammate 3 ---
Procedures:
- Create payment: Initializes and processes a payment transaction for a customer order.
- Finalize order: Completes the order confirmation process and moves it to the appropriate queue.

Triggers:
- After order insert: Executes logic immediately after a new order is placed (e.g., notifying restaurant/rider).
- After payment insert: Executes logic after a payment is successfully recorded (e.g., updating order status).

Reports:
- Rider current delivery report: Shows the active deliveries that are currently being handled by riders.
- Rider delivery report: Details the overall historical delivery performance and records of riders.

--- Teammate 4 ---
Procedures:
- Add subscription: Assigns and activates a new subscription for a customer.
- Add to cart: Inserts selected menu items into a customer's active shopping cart.

Triggers:
- After sub payment insert: Executes logic after a subscription payment is completed (e.g., extending subscription dates).
- Before cart insert: Validates cart items (e.g., checking item availability) before they are added.

Reports:
- Monthly restaurant sales: Shows the aggregated monthly sales revenue for the restaurants.
- Subscription countdown report: Displays customers with a subscription that is ending in 7 days.

--- Teammate 5 ---
Procedures:
- Complete order (Complete delivery): Marks an order as successfully delivered and finalized.
- Add rider: Registers a new delivery rider into the system.

Triggers:
- Before rider update: Validates or tracks changes before a rider's information (like status or location) is updated.
- After rider insert: Executes logic immediately after a new rider is added to the system.

Reports:
- Customer retention report: Analyzes and displays customer retention metrics and return rates.
- Average order value report: Calculates total spending and average spending of customers, grouped by their loyalty tier.

--- Queries (Purpose) ---
- average-order-value.sql: Retrieves total order amount and average order value by loyalty tier (Diamond, Gold, Silver) for spending analysis.
- coin-flow.sql: Shows monthly coin issuance and redemption totals by customer segment/tier to track coin economy movement.
- coin-transaction.sql: Displays detailed customer coin transaction history (balance, change amount, related order, and transaction timestamp).
- loyalty-benefits.sql: Lists loyalty tiers with minimum spend requirement and corresponding benefit types/values for program reference.
- loyalty-customer.sql: Ranks customers within each loyalty tier based on total spent, useful for customer value analysis.
- menu-of-open-restaurant.sql: Returns menu items from currently open restaurants with filters like halal status, type, price, budget, and super deal flags.
- most-ordered-menu.sql: Identifies top ordered menu items by total quantity sold across restaurants.
- order-volume.sql: Summarizes delivered order volume by platform to compare platform delivery performance.
- restaurant-revenue.sql: Shows restaurant-level sales performance including total items sold and total revenue.
- subscription-countdown.sql: Lists subscribed users and days left before subscription expiry for renewal monitoring.

--- Query Decision-Making Mapping (Strategic / Tactical / Operational) ---
- average-order-value.sql
	Strategic: Evaluate long-term customer segment profitability by tier for loyalty program redesign.
	Tactical: Tune monthly promotions and discount depth by tier based on spending behavior.
	Operational: Monitor current AOV trend by tier to detect sudden drops in order value.

- coin-flow.sql
	Strategic: Assess sustainability of coin ecosystem (issued vs redeemed) across customer segments.
	Tactical: Adjust campaign coin rewards and redemption rules each month/quarter.
	Operational: Track recent month coin flow anomalies for immediate corrective actions.

- coin-transaction.sql
	Strategic: Identify long-term coin usage patterns and fraud-risk behavior categories.
	Tactical: Refine wallet rules, redemption limits, and customer support workflows.
	Operational: Investigate day-to-day suspicious or disputed coin transactions quickly.

- loyalty-benefits.sql
	Strategic: Redesign tier architecture and benefit economics based on value delivery.
	Tactical: Update tier benefit mix to improve conversion from Silver to Gold/Diamond.
	Operational: Verify active tier benefits shown to users are consistent with current policy.

- loyalty-customer.sql
	Strategic: Identify high-value customer clusters for retention and VIP strategy.
	Tactical: Target win-back or upsell campaigns at specific customers within each tier.
	Operational: Generate current top-customer list for immediate engagement activities.

- menu-of-open-restaurant.sql
	Strategic: Understand long-term assortment and partner coverage opportunities.
	Tactical: Optimize menu placement and category campaigns for open restaurants.
	Operational: Support live menu visibility decisions (what to show/highlight now).

- most-ordered-menu.sql
	Strategic: Decide long-term partnership focus around consistently high-demand items.
	Tactical: Plan bundles and cross-sell campaigns based on frequent best-sellers.
	Operational: Promote top-selling items in daily featured slots.

- order-volume.sql
	Strategic: Evaluate platform/channel performance for expansion or consolidation decisions.
	Tactical: Rebalance delivery resources and incentive programs by platform.
	Operational: Monitor current delivered order counts to manage daily workload.

- restaurant-revenue.sql
	Strategic: Identify key revenue-driving restaurants for long-term account strategy.
	Tactical: Prioritize merchant support, commissions, and promotions by revenue band.
	Operational: Track current revenue ranking to trigger immediate merchant actions.

- subscription-countdown.sql
	Strategic: Measure long-term subscription retention risk and renewal behavior.
	Tactical: Schedule reminder cadence and targeted renewal offers before expiry.
	Operational: Execute daily outreach to users with low remaining subscription days.
